More About Me.

An Infusion of Composition in Design

Hello I am Madikela Dikgale. A 24 year old Graphic Designer & Visual Artist. I have a unique perspective about life, think of life in a way that people wonder what kind of person I am. The simple things about life seems to flabbergast me. Simple, minimal, clean defines the kind of person I am. I implement the minimalism look and feel in my work.

Passion driven by art, motivated by the beauty of this Earth. A ghost that never sleeps. Graphic Designer. Photographer. Conceptualist. Visualist. That is what I want to be and I am getting there.
